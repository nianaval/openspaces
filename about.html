About Open Spaces Boston

Open Spaces Boston is an interactive map and guide of the various open spaces—parks, fields, reservations, gardens, plazas, and even cemeteries—in Boston, MA. The website features filters for the different types of spaces a Bostonian may be looking for, as well as details on any space that is clicked.

This was a final project for a Programming Basics class I took at Northeastern University with Professor Nathan Melenbrink in the fall of 2017, and includes the use of HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Google Maps Javascript API.
